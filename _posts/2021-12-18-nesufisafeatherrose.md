Forest of red flows
Even as the sun lowers.
After night hours
The red sun falls from the sky
Half the night is black. 

(2)
Ne sufisa Feather Rose.
Avec chaveau de rouge et noir. 

Red sun in the sky
Reddens the clouds in sky.
Only the voice is
Slowly whispering to me.
Enjoy coolness 

throughout the year.
