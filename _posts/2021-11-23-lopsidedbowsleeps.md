Lopsided bow red
and frilly, tilted
leftward.
Her shirt the color
of the sunset sky.
Shadows
in the crypt lurking,
seeking. They who
haunt 

the winter sunset
make their bets sitting
down, long in the crypt. 

Midnight she sleeps
Her power now beckons me
to the darkest night.
